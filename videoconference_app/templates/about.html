About v-conf
A hassle-free, modern video chat platform
v-conf is an intuitive and seamless web-based video chat application, designed to make connecting, collaborating, and communicating easier than ever. Inspired by the need for secure, reliable, and user-friendly video conferencing, v-conf provides a frictionless way to host or join meetings with just a click—no downloads or complex setups required.
  • Instant Meetings: Create or join video meetings in seconds.
  • Modern UI: Clean, responsive, and visually appealing interface with a premium blue palette.
  • Secure & Private: Meetings are protected and your data is respected.
  • Accessible Everywhere: Works on any modern device—desktop, tablet, or mobile.
  • Easy Authentication: Simple sign-up and sign-in with minimal hassle.
Built with: Python (Django), HTML, CSS, JavaScript, WebRTC, Bootstrap, FontAwesome, and more.
